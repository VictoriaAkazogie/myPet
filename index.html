My dog

Bimbim is the name of my pet dog. He has long droopy ears and white fur. He has a cute face, brown eyes, little paws and a tiny tail. I got him two years ago from an animal shelter. He runs really fast with his tiny legs and he eats literally anything you give him. When strangers pass by he barks really loudly but never bites. I bathe him once every few days. My pet, Bimbim is close to me and I adore him. We go for a walk every morning. He wags his tail with joy whenever he sees me. I take him to the vet ocasionally for vaccination. I love dogs in general, they are man's best friend.
